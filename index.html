Academic Profile

Researcher • Writer • Philosopher • Epistemologist

Welcome

I am an independent researcher, writer, and interdisciplinary thinker exploring the intersections of philosophy, literature, history, society, artificial intelligence, and scientific inquiry. My work is driven by a fascination with knowledge itself—how it is produced, transmitted, challenged, and transformed across cultures, technologies, and generations. My intellectual interests range from classical and contemporary philosophy to machine learning, astronomy, science communication, and the social implications of emerging technologies. Through essays, research projects, and public scholarship, I seek to bridge the humanities and sciences while making complex ideas accessible to broader audiences.

About

I am interested in questions that lie at the boundaries of disciplines:
Philosophy of knowledge and epistemology Literature and intellectual history Sociology of technology and culture Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Astronomy and space science Science communication and public understanding of knowledge Historical development of ideas and institutions

My long-term goal is to contribute to interdisciplinary research that connects humanistic inquiry with scientific and technological advancement.

Research Interests

Philosophy & Epistemology

Investigating the nature of knowledge, truth, belief, reasoning, and intellectual traditions.

Literature & Intellectual History

Exploring literary thought, classical traditions, cultural narratives, and the historical evolution of ideas.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Studying machine learning systems, human-AI interaction, and the philosophical implications of intelligent technologies.

Astronomy & Space Science

Examining scientific discoveries, cosmology, planetary science, and public engagement with space exploration.

Sociology & Society

Understanding institutions, culture, social change, and the relationship between technology and society.

Science Communication

Translating complex scientific and philosophical ideas into accessible forms for wider audiences.
